    Examples
    
        
    Real scenarios we flag
    
        
    Concrete cases with signals and guidance.
    
      
    
      
    
        
    
          
    Fake delivery text
    
          
    “Your package is held. Confirm payment within 10 minutes: http://quick-drop-off.info”
    
          
      
            
    • Urgency with short deadline
      • 
            
    • Unverified URL with tracking parameters
      • 
            
    • Payment request outside normal carrier flow
      • 
          
    
          
    Verdict: High risk. Action: Ignore and report as phishing.
    
        
    
        
    
          
    Phishing login link
    
          
    “Security alert: login from a new device. Verify at secure-login.example-co.net”
    
          
      
            
    • Lookalike domain with hyphenated brand
      • 
            
    • Requests credentials via redirected page
      • 
            
    • Claims security event without verification path
      • 
          
    
          
    Verdict: High risk. Action: Do not sign in; use the official site or app.
    
        
    
        
    
          
    Job interview scam
    
          
    “We want to hire you today. Send your ID and bank details to process onboarding.”
    
          
      
            
    • Asks for sensitive data before verification
      • 
            
    • Unverified sender domain and no role details
      • 
            
    • Promises immediate hiring without process
      • 
          
    
          
    Verdict: Medium risk. Action: Verify company and role through official channels.
