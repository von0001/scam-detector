Common Questions

• Why does the extension say “Safe” or “Potentially Dangerous”?
The analysis is based on scam patterns, keywords, URL structure, and other signals. It is not perfect—always use judgment.

• Does the extension read my browser tabs?
No. ScamDetector only analyzes content that YOU manually paste in.

• Does the website store what I paste?
No. Analysis happens in real-time and is not stored.
