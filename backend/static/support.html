Common Topics

How scans protect you

ScamDetector looks for scam language, emotional pressure, bad links, QR tricks, and account phishing behavior. Which then turns that into a simple verdict you can act on.

Think a result is wrong?

AI isn’t perfect. If something feels off, tell us. Your feedback helps tighten the system and protect the next person.

Screenshot & QR issues

Blurry, dark, or cropped images can break OCR or QR scanning. Try zooming in, improving lighting, or re-uploading.

Privacy & data safety

Content you scan is processed in-memory and discarded. No sale of data. No tracking of your personal messages.

Still Need Help?

Describe what happened, drop examples if you can, and we’ll look into it. We take scam detection and accuracy very seriously.
