Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Information We Collect

User-submitted content
Anything you paste or upload is processed temporarily to perform scam detection. This data is never stored, logged, or shared.

Minimal usage statistics
We record anonymous usage metrics such as total requests, safe vs scam detections, and OCR usage. No IP addresses, personal identifiers, or tracking fingerprints are collected.

Browser Extension
The extension only analyzes content you explicitly submit. It does not monitor webpages, track activity, or operate without your interaction.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used strictly to generate real-time analysis results. We do not sell, share, or monetize user data in any form.

3. Cookies & Tracking

ScamDetector does not use behavioral tracking, cross-site cookies, or advertising analytics. Session cookies are only used for administrative authentication where explicitly required.

4. Security

All communication with ScamDetector occurs over encrypted HTTPS. Content is processed in-memory and discarded immediately after analysis.

5. Third-Party Services

AI-powered analysis is performed using secure, industry-standard APIs. No personal data is permanently stored or retained by these services.

6. User Rights

You retain full ownership over any content you provide. You may discontinue use of the service at any time without providing personal information.
