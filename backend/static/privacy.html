ScamDetector Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

ScamDetector helps users analyze text, links, and screenshots for potential scams. We take privacy seriously and designed the service to collect as little data as possible.

Information We Collect

1. User-submitted content
Content you paste or upload is processed only to generate scam detection results. It is not stored or shared.

2. Basic usage statistics
We store minimal, non-personal analytics counts (e.g., total requests, scam vs safe counts). No IP addresses or identifiers are logged.

3. Chrome Extension
The extension does not read webpages, does not run automatically, and only analyzes text you manually paste.

How Your Data Is Used

We do not sell data, share data, or use data for advertising. All data is used only to provide scam detection functionality.

Cookies & Tracking

No cookies, ads, tracking scripts, or behavioral analytics are used.

Security

All content is transmitted securely over HTTPS and discarded immediately after processing.
